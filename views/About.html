Welcome to ClickTrust!

Meet the future computer scientists of tomorrow.
We are Computer Science students at the College of Management. Our journey to creating this website began with a personal experience where we unsuccessfully tried to sell concert tickets we could not attend. Recognizing this as a problem in need of a solution, we came up with the idea for this platform. We applied the skills we gained throughout our studies into building this site to provide our users with a more reliable and innovative experience in an area that can often be challenging to navigate. We're here to ensure that everyone has the opportunity to buy and sell digital assets online in a safe and secure way.

Thank you for visiting!
